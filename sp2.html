  • Student Public Service Application Details
    1. What you will do?
    2. Who can apply?

      Junior High School student and above.

    3. How long can I service?

      You can service 3 hours or less in one time and one day. The time and location that is able to apply are noted on the sheet below. Actual service sections are arranged according to the registration status and the needs of the library.

    4. How to apply?

      You can apply through online form below, by phone or email, or physically come to the check-in place.

    5. 5. Notice
      1. (1) The library provides reservation for service in next 30 days, which need to be registered in advance, and served according to the scheduled time.
      2. (2) For Student under 18, please fill in and sign the Parent Consent form, and give it to the staff on the day of public service.
      3. (3) The application will be reviewed by the receiving unit, and then the actual service period will be notified. The library reserves the right to accept or decline the application.
      4. (4) Please ensure to maintain a clean and tidy appearance during public service.
